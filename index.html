A technology that is capable of providing an environment which enables users to transmit data from one device to other device without the use of any physical link/cables is known as Mobile Computing Mobile Communication is the framework that is responsible behind the working of mobile computing technology. Mobile communication framework includes communication devise such as mobiles, laptops, as rules of conduct, fitness etc. They are responsible for delivering of smooth communication process. -Some of the major field in which mobile computing can be applied are: -Web or Internet access. -Global Position System (GPS). -Emergency services. -Entertainment services -Educational services.
